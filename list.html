Edubase, by the Department of Education

GB-EDU

All educational establishments in the United Kingdom must be registered with the Department of Education. Edubase is provided by the Department of Education and provides a URN for each school, university and other educational establishment in England and Wales. However, the EduBase CSV contains 4,248 records, and therefore cannot be said to be a complete dataset for all educational establishments in England and Wales.
